# 🗝️ Escape the Room 🕵️‍♂️

Welcome to **Escape the Room** – a thrilling, text-based adventure game where your wits are your only way out! Can you solve the puzzles and make your way to freedom? 🧩🚪

---

## 🎮 Features

- 🏠 **Multiple Rooms:** Challenge yourself with a series of uniquely designed rooms, each with its own secrets.
- 🧠 **Mind-Bending Puzzles:** Crack codes, find hidden items, and connect clues to progress.
- 📦 **Inventory System:** Collect and use objects like keys, wires, and tools to unlock new mysteries.
- 🎨 **Retro ASCII Art:** Enjoy immersive visuals through creative ASCII room art for that old-school charm!
- 🎹 **Keyboard Controls:** Navigate with ease using `W` (forward), `A` (left), `D` (right), and `Enter` (go back/interact).
- 💡 **Hints Everywhere:** Pay attention! The art, text, and your inventory often hide the answers you seek.

---

## 🚦 How to Play

1. **Start the Game:**  
   Run `main.py` in your Python terminal.

2. **Controls:**
    - `D` ➡️ Go right
    - `A` ⬅️ Go left
    - `W` ⬆️ Go forward
    - `Enter` ↩️ Go back or interact

3. **Explore & Solve:**  
   - Interact with objects (look for prompts with `>`).
   - Enter passwords, collect items, and use logic to escape.
   - Each room has its own twist. Don’t be afraid to experiment!

4. **Win:**  
   Solve all puzzles and unlock the final door to escape! 🏆
